About Inlec Solutions Australia Pty Ltd

Inlec Solutions Australia is a forward-thinking electrical and instrumentation specialist. Operating out of Springfield Lakes, Queensland, we focus purely on the industrial sectors—bridging the gap between complex engineering requirements and practical, actionable on-site solutions.
