About Inlec Solutions

Built on Precision.
Trusted by Industry.

Inlec Solutions Australia Pty Ltd is an Analytical Devices Engineering Services company based in Springfield Lakes, Queensland. Over more than five years of operation, our team — led by Director Devakumar Jayakumar — has delivered precision commissioning, calibration, and field services to critical industrial facilities.

With a combined industry experience of over 60 years, we bring deep technical knowledge across all major analyzer platforms to every engagement. Our core strengths set us apart from generalist providers.
